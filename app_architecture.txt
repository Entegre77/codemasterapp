The CodeMaster Development Ai APP will have a user-friendly interface that is easy to navigate and use. The key features and functionality of the APP will include:
- Code suggestions and feedback powered by OpenAI Codex
- Code completion and prediction
- Code review assistance
- Bug detection and code quality analysis
- Automatic code refactoring

The APP will be designed to meet the needs of developers and programmers of all skill levels, from beginners to experts. It will be highly customizable and will allow users to tailor the APP to meet their specific needs and preferences.
