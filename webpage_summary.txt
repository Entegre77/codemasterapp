The steps to find the URL of a website are: go to a search engine, type in what you're looking for, review the search results, click on a link to view the website, find the URL in the address bar, and copy it if needed. Alternatively, you can right-click on the website's link and select "Copy link address" to copy the URL to your clipboard. On a phone or tablet, you can tap and hold the place you want to paste the URL and then tap "Paste" when it appears on the menu.
